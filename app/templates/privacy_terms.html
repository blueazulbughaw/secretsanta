Text Messaging (SMS) Terms & Opt-In

GiftCircle uses your phone number only to sign you in. By entering your phone number and requesting a sign-in code, you agree to receive an SMS text message from GiftCircle containing a one-time 6-digit passcode.

  • Purpose: account authentication only (no marketing texts).
  • Message frequency: one message per sign-in attempt, roughly a few times per month.
  • Message and data rates may apply from your mobile carrier.
  • Opt out: reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts. You won't be able to sign in by phone afterward.
  • Help: reply HELP for assistance, or contact us using the information below.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy Policy

GiftCircle is a small family gift-exchange app. We collect only what's needed to run it:

  • Phone number — used solely to send your sign-in code. Never shown to other family members.
  • Name — shown to your family group so people know who you are.

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your phone number is used only by our SMS provider (Twilio) to deliver sign-in codes, and by us to identify your account.

You can ask us to delete your account and data at any time using the contact info below.
